The project

PSCAA is the thesis for the Computer Engeneering course of the students Gabriel Carrico Guerrero and Lucas Felipe Ribeiro with the orientation of the Prof. Dr. Nádia Puchalski Kozievitch.
Every day a considerable ammount of accidents happen on the brasilian federal roadways. Part of these accidents requires Aerial Ambulances, with pre-determined flight plans and hospitals to arrive in the shortest ammount of time possible. In this direction, this thesis has as the objective to build a prototype that helps the rescuers and doctors to find the best route and the closest hospital. The project takes advantage of databases, interaction human-computer and algorithm analises. As a result, it is expected to build an application with a friendly interface, usefull to the user.
